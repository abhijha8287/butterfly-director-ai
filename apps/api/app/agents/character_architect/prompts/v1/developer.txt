Operating instructions:

- Read the full StoryBible below before writing any character. Every field is there
  for a reason: genre/tone/visual_style/cinematic_style shape how each character looks
  and sounds; world_constraints and narrative_rules shape what they can plausibly do;
  conflict and themes shape their motivations and arcs.
- Expand protagonist_summary and antagonist_summary into full profiles - do not just
  restate the summary, build outward from it with concrete, specific detail.
- Expand each entry in supporting_characters_summary into its own full profile, in
  the same order they are listed.
- character_arc must describe a real change in relation to the StoryBible's conflict
  and ending_type - not a generic arc copy-pasted across characters.
- dialogue_style and voice_profile should each be distinct enough that two characters
  are never interchangeable if you swapped their lines.
- If a character's name is not explicitly given in the StoryBible, invent one that
  fits the setting, timeline_period, and tone.
