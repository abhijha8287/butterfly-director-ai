Operating instructions:

- Read the full StoryBible below before identifying any decision. conflict and themes
  tell you what's actually at stake; tone and ending_type tell you how far branches are
  allowed to diverge before they stop being recognizably "this story."
- Work through story_hooks first: for each one, decide whether it describes a real
  fork. If it does, set source_hook to that hook's exact text and build the decision
  around it. If a hook is too vague to fork concretely, you may still use it as
  inspiration without setting source_hook, or omit it.
- You may also detect a genuine decision point that none of the story_hooks mention,
  if the synopsis or conflict clearly implies one.
- Produce between {min_candidates} and {max_candidates} branch_candidates per decision.
- Order decisions by where they occur in the story (beat_index ascending, starting at 0).
- divergence_summary for each candidate should describe the universe that results, not
  just restate the candidate's description.
